Fonts Used

The CSS for this page links to your local downloads of

  • Source Serif Pro (body)
  • Macondo Swash Caps (main header)
  • Headland One (all other headers)
  • Libre Baskeville (quotes)

The setup is quick for me because I already have those fonts downloaded! See it in context.

You can replicate this look by downloading the fonts from The Google Webfonts Helper - or replace them with google webfonts code. And of course, swap out the fonts for your own

Summer

Summer is my favourite time of year. Here's a quote from The Great Gatsby

“And so with the sunshine and the great bursts of leaves growing on the trees, just as things grow in fast movies, I had that familiar conviction that life was beginning over again with the summer.”

The Great Gatsby is what we call a book. This fancy quotes functionality puts in elegant curly quotation marks, which look nicer than 'the standard ones' you get while typing.
